# Team Collaboration & Strategy

*This document outlines our organizational framework, tool selection, and internal evaluation processes.*

---

## 1. Project & Communication Tools
We adopted a "Documentation as Code" approach to keep our development and management in sync.

* **GitHub:** Serves as our "Single Source of Truth". We use it for version control and the **GitHub Project Board (Kanban)** to visualize our workflow.
* **VS Code:** Our standardized IDE. This ensures that all team members use the same linting and markdown rendering, minimizing formatting conflicts.
* **Discord/Teams:** Used for quick ad-hoc communication and weekly sync-meetings.

## 2. Decision Making & Conflict Management
To ensure progress and team harmony, we established a clear organizational framework:

* **Scientific Perspective:** We use a **Consensus-Based Decision Model**. For major technical shifts, we require a brief discussion in the weekly meeting.
* **Conflict Resolution:** In case of ties or disagreements, we use a "Majority Vote" or consult the project owner (Client/Tutor).
* **Ownership:** Tasks are assigned via GitHub Issues, giving each member clear responsibility for their domain.
